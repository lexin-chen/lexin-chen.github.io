About

Welcome!

Lexin is a Chemistry Ph.D. student at the University of Florida with a focus on computational chemistry and cheminformatics. Her current research interest is building algorithms to analyze molecular similarity of large datasets using extended similar indices and exploring drug discovery applications.

Outside of research, she is devoted to outreach toward diversity in STEM and stimulating scientific curiosity.

Technical Skills

  • Python: Numpy, Seaborns, Multiprocessing, Pandas, etc.
  • Molecular dynamics simulation on AMBER and NAMD software packages.
  • Molecular dynamics enhanced sampling: Weighted Ensemble using WESPA.
  • Quantum calculations on Gaussian software package.
  • Fluent in Mandarin and Cantonese.
